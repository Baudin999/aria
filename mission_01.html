What’s up Wythburn

Wythburn is named after Wythburn or as he was called by the southerners “Witchburn”. Witchburn fought in the last great war against the northern blight. Northmen came down from Scandinavia to raid the isles.

Lore: 100

Our heroes wake up in a farm in Wythburn in a shed. The nameplate states: Alan Bean. Our heroes need to escape the barn. Alan and some other men and women come in and grab a woman who is still unconscious and drag her out of the barn. After some screaming the sounds die out.
